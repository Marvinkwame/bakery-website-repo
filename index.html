ABOUT US


Bakery Palace is a modern communal bakery and pastry company, producing honest and nutritious bread, pastry and food. All bread and pastry are made by hand, from scratch using home learnt techniques and pure Ghanaian sourced ingredients


We aspire to continue serving our customers with unique and tailor- made pastries and other customized food based on their budgets and needs. We are collectively concentrating our efforts on learning and developing new recipes which will meet the health needs of our clients, as well as growing and scaling up the company. Over the course of the production, we have been challenging every single step. In search of a unique experience. Our sweet team has been sharing and discussing the tastes and knowledge with our quality-demanding food experts.
